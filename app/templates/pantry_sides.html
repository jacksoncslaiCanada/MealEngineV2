Pantry Sides Guide
8 go-to sides ready in 25 minutes or less — no extra planning needed.
1
Steamed Rice
15–20 min
White, brown, or jasmine — rinse, add to a pot with a 1:2 rice-to-water ratio, bring to a boil, then simmer covered for 15 min. Pairs with almost any main.
Rice cooker? Even easier. Set and forget.
2
Roasted Broccoli
22 min
Cut into florets, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 425°F (220°C) for 20–22 min until edges are crisp and golden. Works with any protein.
Add a squeeze of lemon and parmesan at the end.
3
Simple Green Salad
5 min
Mixed greens, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, and a drizzle of olive oil with lemon or your favourite dressing. No cook. Fresh contrast to any rich main.
Keep greens pre-washed in the fridge — weeknight ready.
4
Garlic Butter Noodles
12 min
Cook egg noodles or spaghetti, drain, then toss with butter, minced garlic, a splash of pasta water, and fresh parsley. Simple, satisfying, kid-approved.
Toast the garlic in butter for 60 seconds before tossing.
5
Sautéed Spinach
5 min
Heat olive oil in a wide pan over medium-high. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds, then add spinach. Toss for 3–4 min until just wilted. Season with salt and a pinch of nutmeg.
One large bag of spinach cooks down to 2 servings.
6
Roasted Baby Potatoes
30 min
Halve baby potatoes, toss with olive oil, rosemary (fresh or dried), salt, and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 25–30 min until golden and tender inside.
Cut them smaller for faster cooking on a weeknight.
7
Steamed Bok Choy
6 min
Halve baby bok choy lengthwise. Steam or blanch for 4–5 min until tender-crisp. Drizzle with sesame oil, soy sauce, and a pinch of chili flakes. Especially great with Asian mains.
Substitute broccolini or gai lan when bok choy isn't available.
8
Pearl Couscous
10 min
Toast couscous in a dry pan for 2 min, then add boiling water (1:1.5 ratio), cover, and cook for 8 min. Fluff with a fork, drizzle with olive oil. Neutral and fluffy — pairs beautifully with stews and braises.
Regular couscous works too — just pour boiling water and cover for 5 min.
All sides use pantry and produce staples — no additional shopping required on most weeks.
